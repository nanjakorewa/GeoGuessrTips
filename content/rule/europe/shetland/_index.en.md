{{% notice tip %}}
Trees—even shrubs—are scarce{{% ex "https://goo.gl/maps/rxQ3kGiEsjgKVVPE6" "https://goo.gl/maps/bCSF9qFXTTjDXiER9" %}}. Like the rest of Scotland you will find “passing place” signs. If the landscape feels very rocky, it might actually be mainland Scotland{{% goto "https://goo.gl/maps/KoisKnBkeSPZSgL77" "as in this example" %}}.

{{% notice tip %}}
Cattle grids—metal bars laid across the road—are frequent{{% ref "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cattle_grid" "Cattle grid" %}}. They keep sheep and cattle from wandering out of the grazing area. You will see them in many pastoral regions, but Shetland has a particularly high density{{% ex "https://goo.gl/maps/q6wJJ7gYiXbnie7x6" "https://goo.gl/maps/NpvFXScYFSmwmu4x8" "https://goo.gl/maps/cw7DPJTFpF1H16Sf8" %}}.
