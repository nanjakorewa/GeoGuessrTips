    <h2 class="section-title">{{% i18n "how-to-distinguish" %}}</h2>
    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>The country comprises two entities: the Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina, and Republika Srpska.</li>

{{% tabs %}}
{{% tab "w-etf" %}}
{{% notice tip %}}
A country of steep karst terrain: towns and roads hug trunk routes, railways, and rivers{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/oMumB1NqGks19b2NA" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/vRfSM3B6E5sXWPvx8" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/9xUAGyFH1TzUHWJv6" %}}. Large tunnels for straight highways are costly and tricky, so many roads meander along the valley floor{{% ref "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drina" "Drina River meanders" %}}. You often see beautiful green rivers{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/tcTfe9Vfk4opn7jS9" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/UpMAdd4snR22pnQZA" %}}.

<div class="main-desciption area-description">
    <h2 class="section-title">{{% i18n "narrow-down-the-area" %}}</h2>
    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>The Dayton Agreement (General Framework Agreement for Peace in Bosnia and Herzegovina) defined two administrative entities
            <ul>
                <li>Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina, where Croats and Bosniaks are the majority
                    <ul>
                        <li>House nameplates and similar signage are mainly in <b>Latin script</b>{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/1SMj5TdsdYDTA2BU9" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/fiPmpYmGztg8PHWn8" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/szeSL8KugWactQHn6" %}}</li>
                        <li>You may see homes flying the blue and yellow flag{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/EmEcJ5MELy3FkgAAA" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/8zd6C6ezQV5Wmfun6" %}}</li>
                        <li>Area codes include 061 / 062 / 063{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/SGYfkhFaVDp7Gy387" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/85gGx3L9niyiwCWw9" %}}</li>
                    </ul>
                </li>
                <li>Republika Srpska, where Serbs are the majority
                    <ul>
                        <li>House nameplates are mainly in <b>Cyrillic script</b>{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/Z9UGA6ZuBhrFjtuW9" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/YT1MC5ifuRueeR2L8" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/nDxxDvaLNy6Fet1H8" %}}</li>
                        <li>The coat of arms of Republika Srpska can be spotted{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/oHLeo6kxdrjWQbhKA" %}}</li>
                        <li>Houses may fly the horizontal red-blue-white tricolor{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/ctoXTty8nME3FVqSA" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/nZ1R29J3m6hfz7AL7" %}}</li>
                        <li>Area codes include 065 / 066{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/yGFGmnuk77JhdsWm9" %}}</li>
                        <li>Large roadside signs appear more frequently{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/2nvFtD9u6kttWvJs5" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/9zkUKJLDQ2Fg8BGu6" %}}</li>
                    </ul>
                </li>
                <li>Some bilingual signs have one language spray-painted over or vandalized{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/aHFfJjLQyQSoWCQM6" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/4kVnatPx2oi6HZKX9" %}}; a similar phenomenon occurs on Corsica in {{% goto "../france/" "France" %}}.</li>
            </ul>
        </li>
        <li>The Dinaric Alps cut across the country, giving the south higher and rugged terrain
            <ul>
                <li>Northern plains{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/JFkHZAFxU3EHZsWV7" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/Be8bvpAKP2GnoG9C9" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/EyPK4Tnf2GwwwRwLA" %}}</li>
                <li>Dinaric Alps{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/ZsvbxwYn9QmUmj7W8" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/wwTJijisUmjAeCpb6" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/gtnF9T1mHLZMiRzV7" %}}</li>
                <li>Southern areas{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/EwKFMYhbrshuQdZh7" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/4hQGa8CV9CFRHky76" %}}; near the Neretva river mouth you can feel a Mediterranean climate{{% ref "https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E3%83%8D%E3%83%AC%E3%83%88%E3%83%B4%E3%82%A1%E5%B7%9D" "Neretva River" %}}</li>
            </ul>
        </li>
        <li>Many railways run along rivers
            <ul>
                <li>Lines southwest of Sarajevo{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/pknJc9DQrtmbdbLu5" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/5tBABHPSGgMxPSgV6" %}}</li>
            </ul>
        </li>
    </ul>

{{% tab "Topography" %}}
{{% notice tip %}}
The Dinaric Alps spread across the south and include areas over 2,000 m in elevation. The north has a continental climate, while coastal-adjacent southern areas shift toward a Mediterranean climate.
{{% /notice %}}
