<div class="main-desciption country-description">
    <h2 class="section-title">{{% i18n "how-to-distinguish" %}}</h2>
    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>Country-code top-level domain is <span class="quiz">.ad</span></li>
        <li>Green roadside bollards often run down the <span class="quiz">centre line</span> or shoulder</li>
        <li>License plates have a <span class="quiz">dark band on the left</span></li>
        <li>Mountainous surroundings with stone walls and patterned apartment blocks are common</li>
        <li>Red-tipped snow poles stand along the roadside</li>
        <li>Pedestrian crossing signs share Spain’s eight-bar design, so pay close attention</li>
        <li>Ski resorts and snow scenes appear frequently {{% ex "https://goo.gl/maps/v1yo8Q4Umb3RA3JR8" "https://goo.gl/maps/7jyT3MVvjAWxjXaN6" "https://goo.gl/maps/WD6r8gtptfgGwsGx8" "https://goo.gl/maps/AshXK6YnGBBWq2rz7" %}}</li>
    </ul>

{{% notice tip %}}
Expect steep mountains and buildings faced with stone cladding. {{% ex "https://goo.gl/maps/HTiQdHf8CtJ7q3RD7" "https://goo.gl/maps/1gnf9YFxHNpJjxCd6" %}} Even in town centres you can usually glimpse a slope between buildings. {{% ex "https://goo.gl/maps/uCdX73HmturK9a467" %}}
{{% /notice %}}

{{% notice info %}}
Similar alpine architecture appears in the western Italian Alps {{% ex "https://goo.gl/maps/uSRbQsrbskQkajcb6" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/VceHovBcdNMLTTEo7" %}} and on the Spanish side around Andorra {{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/xuUma9CQXosXWaoZA" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/6PqHJCuxAcjhtbs96" %}}, so double-check before locking in a guess.
{{% /notice %}}
