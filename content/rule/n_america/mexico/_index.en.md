    <h2 class="section-title">{{% i18n "how-to-distinguish" %}}</h2>
        <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>The domain is <span class="quiz">.mx</span></li>
        <li>Language is <span class="quiz">Spanish</span></li>
        <li><span class="quiz">OXXO</span> is a major convenience store chain</li>
        <li>Many utility poles are <span class="quiz">octagonal</span></li>
        <li>Major companies often end with <span class="quiz">-MEX</span> or <span class="quiz">-MX</span> (PEMEX, CEMEX, CINEMEX)</li>
        <li>White-and-orange bollards are common</li>
        <li>You can spot a blue <span class="quiz">VeTV</span> antenna on houses</li>
        <li>Stop signs often say <span class="quiz">ALTO</span> (also seen in Guatemala, Panama, and Costa Rica)</li>
    </ul>

    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>State names and highway shields help narrow the region</li>
        <li>The first digit of phone area codes hints at region: <span class="quiz">2-9</span>; even numbers trend north, odd numbers trend south</li>
        <li>Key area codes:
            <ul>
                <li><span class="quiz">55</span> - Mexico City{{% ex "https://goo.gl/maps/p49bh8pfzd8YDKXb6" %}}</li>
                <li><span class="quiz">614</span> - Chihuahua{{% ex "https://goo.gl/maps/pAAQSW5yEdkTvBNa7" %}}</li>
                <li><span class="quiz">656</span> - Ciudad Juarez{{% ex "https://goo.gl/maps/7Z2irAk2QCcD8cha8" %}}</li>
                <li><span class="quiz">663/664</span> - Tijuana{{% ex "https://goo.gl/maps/x8jo1zW64VvTDQmi9" %}}</li>
                <li><span class="quiz">686</span> - Mexicali{{% ex "https://goo.gl/maps/yyFsgPQSjtJbpEiaA" %}}</li>
            </ul>
        </li>
        <li class="no-evidence">Areas near the US sometimes show 000-000-0000 style numbers{{% by "now_and_jenn"  "twitch" %}}</li>
    </ul>
