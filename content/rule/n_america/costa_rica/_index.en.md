    <h2 class="section-title">{{% i18n "how-to-distinguish" %}}</h2>
    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>The domain is <span class="quiz">.co.cr</span>.</li>
        <li>Vehicles drive on the <span class="quiz">right</span>.</li>
        <li>License plates are usually <span class="quiz">white</span> with <span class="quiz">blue</span> text.</li>
        <li>Stops that read "<span class="quiz">ALTO</span>" point to Costa Rica, {{% goto "../mexico/" "Mexico" %}}, {{% goto "../guatemala/" "Guatemala" %}}, or {{% goto "../panama/" "Panama" %}}.</li>
        <li>Volcanoes create many areas of dark soil.</li>
        <li>Signposts are silver, often with a cross brace on the back{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/QPrsf8ez8ghpRqL46" %}}.</li>
    </ul>
