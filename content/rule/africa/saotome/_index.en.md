{{% tab "São Tomé Island" %}}

{{% notice tip %}}If you can see a black Google car, yellow taxis, tall mountains, or a divided road even in the countryside, it is more likely São Tomé Island. Cocoa, coffee, and sugarcane plantations are also more common on São Tomé. The population is larger and the highest peak exceeds 2,000m.
{{% /notice %}}
