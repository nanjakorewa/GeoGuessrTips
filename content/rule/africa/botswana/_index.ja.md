    <h2 class="section-title">{{% i18n "how-to-distinguish" %}}</h2>
    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>Cars drive on the <span class="quiz">left side</span> of the road</li>
        <li>The domain is <span class="quiz">.bw</span></li>
        <li>License plates are often yellow, blue, and white</li>
        <li>Road numbers start with the letters "<span class="quiz">A</span>" or "<span class="quiz">B</span>"</li>
        <li>Signs and guardrails often feature <span class="quiz">yellow and black</span> stripes</li>
        <li>Roads have a rough surface, and shoulders are <span class="quiz">orange</span>, though some areas lack shoulders</li>
        <li class="no-evidence">70% of the country overlaps with the Kalahari Desert, making the terrain mostly flat</li>
        <li class="no-evidence">Older road signs sometimes have a <span class="quiz">blue</span> background</li>
    </ul>

{{% notice tip %}}
Yellow, blue, and white license plates {{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/vW2UDexepSRXxtdT6" %}} are commonly found, with standard passenger vehicles most often displaying <span class="quiz">yellow</span> plates. Some public service vehicles and taxis have <span class="quiz">blue</span> plates on the rear. Additionally, guardrails and signposts often feature <span class="quiz">yellow</span> and <span class="quiz">black</span> stripes {{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/1pUjkwCxyRGCYcGH7" %}}.
