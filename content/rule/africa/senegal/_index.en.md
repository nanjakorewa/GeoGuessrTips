    <h2 class="section-title">{{% i18n "how-to-distinguish" %}}</h2>
    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>The domain is <span class="quiz">.sn</span></li>
        <li>Languages are <span class="quiz">French</span> and Wolof</li>
        <li>Street names often include "RUE" like in {{% goto "../../europe/france/" "France" %}}</li>
        <li>Right-hand traffic, like France</li>
        <li>French-style bollards and utility poles can be found</li>
        <li>License plates are often fully <span class="quiz">blue</span> or have a <span class="quiz">blue</span> left band</li>
        <li>Truck roofs often appear; their types can even identify the region{{% by %}}</li>
        <li class="no-evidence">2015 coverage often shows <span class="quiz">crack-like lines in the sky</span>{{% ex "https://goo.gl/maps/7nwXozV3Yv5RvAph6" "https://goo.gl/maps/XqTzitvRPXjxrjEJ6" %}}</li>

    <ul class="rule-list">
