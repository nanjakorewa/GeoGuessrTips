<div class="main-desciption country-description">
    <h2 class="section-title">{{% i18n "how-to-distinguish" %}}</h2>
    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>Drives on the <span class="quiz">right</span>.</li>
        <li>Country domain: <span class="quiz">.rw</span></li>
        <li>Roads look tidy thanks to community work (<span class="quiz">Umuganda</span>).</li>
        <li>Plates often: <span class="quiz">white front</span>, <span class="quiz">yellow rear</span>.</li>
        <li>Mobile money outlets (e.g., <span class="quiz">MTN</span>) are common in town.</li>
        <li>Square‑section utility poles can appear.</li>
        <li>Google car look is distinctive in many areas.</li>
    </ul>
</div>
