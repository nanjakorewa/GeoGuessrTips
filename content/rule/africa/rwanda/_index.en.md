<div class="main-desciption country-description">
    <h2 class="section-title">{{% i18n "how-to-distinguish" %}}</h2>
    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>Cars drive on the <span class="quiz">right</span></li>
        <li>The domain is <span class="quiz">.rw</span></li>
        <li>Roads are clean with little trash and well‑trimmed grass</li>
        <li>License plates are slim: <span class="quiz">white</span> front and <span class="quiz">yellow</span> rear</li>
        <li>Mobile payment is widespread; kiosks for operators like MTN are common</li>
        <li>Square utility poles appear</li>
        <li>Distinctive Google car</li>
    </ul>
</div>

{{% notice tip %}}In Rwanda, a mandatory community service called Umuganda includes road cleaning, mowing, and trimming street trees each week{{% ref "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Umuganda" "Umuganda" %}}. As a result, roads look relatively clean for Africa. There is little trash{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/BNJ8TsYNd1vuxFcTA" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/TrwV5Eef5at2EQkM6" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/wPExCyEJLQ51RjJw8" %}}, and even unpaved roads often have neatly cut grass{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/DNr28FbdhqSiQwkQ9" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/VaortNd9suh4qnPB9"  "https://maps.app.goo.gl/WqgccX5XGvA2CYLW8" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/aFo1m4oH7haLFVoe9" %}}.
{{% /notice %}}

{{% notice tip %}}MTN and airtel, Rwanda’s mobile carriers, set up umbrella kiosks in many places{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/h1UEax3LcZENDM5U9" "https://maps.app.goo.gl/wdb8vjUUVTStK96F9" "https://goo.gl/maps/F16NsV6qzHpeSFZr9" %}}. Their logos also appear around town{{% ex "https://maps.app.goo.gl/NXTC4LaLemnRpetk6" %}}. These kiosks serve like ATMs for mobile money{{% ref "https://www.watch.impress.co.jp/docs/series/suzukij/1237778.html" "Rwanda and cashless payments (JP)" %}}. They also operate in {{% goto "../uganda/" "Uganda" %}} and {{% goto "../ghana/" "Ghana" %}}.
