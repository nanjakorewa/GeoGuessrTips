    <h2 class="section-title">{{% i18n "how-to-distinguish" %}}</h2>
    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>Country-code top-level domain is <span class="quiz">.ae</span></li>

        <li>Dubai’s skyline features prominent high-rises</li>

Unlike {{% goto "../qatar/" "Qatar" %}}, terrain in the Emirates is rarely perfectly flat.
