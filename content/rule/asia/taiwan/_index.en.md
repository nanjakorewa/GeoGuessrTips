<div class="main-desciption area-description">
    <h2 class="section-title">{{% i18n "narrow-down-the-area" %}}</h2>
    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>If the letter on the utility pole is closer to "A," you are farther <span class="quiz">north</span>; if it is closer to "W," you are farther <span class="quiz">south</span>{{% ex "https://goo.gl/maps/WZt2wngR4nQeEpHr5" "https://goo.gl/maps/q5a4X2ioDfHNKMmj9" %}}</li>
        <li>Telephone area codes get larger as you go south</li>
        <li>Lots of palm trees usually indicate the south</li>
    </ul>
</div>

{{% tabs %}}
{{% tab "Pole code" %}}
{{% notice tip %}}
The first letter near the base of the pole plate indicates latitude: closer to "A" means farther north, closer to "W" means farther south.
{{% /notice %}}
