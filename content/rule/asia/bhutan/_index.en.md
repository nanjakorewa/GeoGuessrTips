<div class="main-desciption country-description">
    <h2 class="section-title">{{% i18n "how-to-distinguish" %}}</h2>
    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>Vehicles drive on the <span class="quiz">left side</span>.</li>
        <li>The Dzongkha language (རྫོང་ཁ) is used.</li>
        <li>Domain is <span class="quiz">.bt</span>.</li>
        <li>License plates are mostly <span class="quiz">red</span>, with yellow and white also seen.</li>
        <li>Distinctive flags can be observed.{{% ref "https://dragontours.jp/about_religion.html" "About Bhutan's Religion" %}}</li>
        <li>If the Google Car is <span class="quiz">gray</span> and driving on the <span class="quiz">left</span>, it's confirmed to be Bhutan.{{% by "https://www.plonkit.net/bhutan" "plonkit" %}}</li>
        <li>Buildings with many uniformly arranged windows are present.</li>
    </ul>
    {{% rd asia bhutan verysmall %}}
</div>
