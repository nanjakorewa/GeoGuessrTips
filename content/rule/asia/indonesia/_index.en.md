    <h4 class="section-title">Architecture</h4>
    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>Indonesia has many ethnic groups, so building styles vary{{% by "https://youtu.be/6xmUB66kxcA?t=255" "youtube" "10 More Maps You NEED To Know for Geoguessr by zi8gzag" %}}{{% ref "https://id.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kategori:Rumah_adat_di_Indonesia" "Traditional houses in Indonesia" %}}</li>

            <ul>
                <li><b>West Sumatra</b>: Minangkabau traditional houses with curved roofs</li>
                <li><b>North Sumatra</b>: Batak roof styles{{% ex "https://goo.gl/maps/8LaAytkBfHv2o1DBA" %}}</li>
                <li><b>Riau</b>: Lontiok houses with curved roofs, stilted floors, and odd-numbered stairs{{%ref "https://id.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lontiok" "Lontiok" %}}</li>
                <li><b>Banten</b>: Sulah nyanda houses of the Baduy{{% ex "https://goo.gl/maps/Hwvy4wQpRBkR6kF56" %}}{{% ref "https://id.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sulah_nyanda" "Sulah nyanda" %}}</li>

        <li class="no-evidence">Area codes often appear together with place names
            <ul>
                <li>08XX numbers are too generic; avoid hunting every sign. Place names are more useful.</li>
                <li>Ignore 08 and memorize that codes increase counterclockwise starting from Jakarta.</li>
                <li><b>03x: East Java</b></li>
                <li style="margin-left:1em;">031x: {{% goto "https://goo.gl/maps/Wcg6zh1Exxy8ibg9A" "Surabaya" map %}}</li>
                <li><b>036x: Bali</b></li>

                <li style="margin-left:1em;">0380: {{% goto "https://goo.gl/maps/uyBSVg1FvLe9E4qp9" "Kupang" map %}}</li>
                <li><b>07x: South Sumatra</b></li>
                <li style="margin-left:1em;">0751: {{% goto "https://goo.gl/maps/GJ4K6EK44PCbv7o56" "Padang" map %}}</li>

            </ul>
        </li>

            <ul>
