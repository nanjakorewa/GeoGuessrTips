    <h2 class="section-title">{{% i18n "how-to-distinguish" %}}</h2>
    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>The domain is <span class="quiz">.ph</span></li>
        <li>Cars drive on the <span class="quiz">right</span></li>
        <li>Official languages are English and Filipino</li>
        <li>Roofs often look like <span class="quiz">corrugated metal</span>{{% by %}}</li>
        <li>You can spot <span class="quiz">Jollibee</span>, the major fast-food chain</li>
        <li><span class="quiz">Tricycles</span> (motorbike + sidecar) are used as taxis</li>
        <li><span class="quiz">Jeepneys</span>, colorful buses, are common</li>
        <li>Public transport plates are often <span class="quiz">yellow</span>, private cars often <span class="quiz">green</span></li>
        <li class="no-evidence">In Asia, red-and-white chevrons likely mean the Philippines or {{% goto "../srilanka/" "Sri Lanka" %}}</li>
        <li class="no-evidence">Royal Crown Cola is sold</li>
        <li class="no-evidence">Vehicles with no rear door are almost always the Philippines</li>
