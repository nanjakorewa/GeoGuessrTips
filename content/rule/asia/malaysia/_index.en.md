    <h2 class="section-title">{{% i18n "how-to-distinguish" %}}</h2>
    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>The domain is <span class="quiz">.my</span></li>
        <li>Official languages include Malay and English</li>
        <li>Cars drive on the <span class="quiz">left</span></li>
        <li><span class="quiz">Yellow</span> hexagonal route numbers</li>
        <li>Stop signs say <span class="quiz">berhenti</span></li>
        <li>License plates are <span class="quiz">white text on black</span></li>
        <li>If you see a <span class="quiz">black label</span> on utility poles, it is the peninsula{{% by "https://youtu.be/HgPymmvsq8c?t=2551" "youtube" "【GeoGuessr】Country Guide - Daig_O" %}}</li>
        <li>SDN BHD / BHD mean company / public company</li>
        <li>Signs often use black-and-white striped poles</li>
        <li>Roads are mostly asphalt, not concrete</li>
    </ul>

    <h2 class="section-title">{{% i18n "narrow-down-the-area" %}}</h2>
    <ul class="rule-list">
        <li>If you see Jawi script on street names or supermarket signs, it may be the northern or southern peninsula{{% ref "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jawi_script" "Jawi script" %}}.</li>
        <li>Agricultural distribution is uneven
            <ul>
                <li>Oil palm plantations are common on the east side of the peninsula and around Sabah</li>
                <li>Rice paddies are more common in the far north of the peninsula</li>
                <li>Data source: <a href="https://ipad.fas.usda.gov/countrysummary/default.aspx?id=MY">U.S. Malaysia Production Country Summary (USDA)</a></li>
            </ul>
        </li>
        <li>Refer to <a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1BYu38SKT5Gej-jjHZ0cESvmRGPGT1KqaBpRgQa3Ctz0/edit">The Malaysia Doc by zi8gzag</a></li>
    </ul>
</div>

{{% tab "Jawi script" %}}
{{% notice tip %}}
Common in the northern peninsula (Terengganu, Kelantan, Kedah, Perlis) and the southern peninsula (Johor, the state closest to Singapore). If the sign is yellow, it is likely <span class="quiz">Johor</span>{{% ref "https://docs.google.com/document/d/1BYu38SKT5Gej-jjHZ0cESvmRGPGT1KqaBpRgQa3Ctz0/edit" "The Malaysia Doc by zi8gzag" %}}. Jawi script also appears in Indonesia’s Riau and Riau Islands.
{{% /notice %}}
