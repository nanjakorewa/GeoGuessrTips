Bangladesh

How to Distinguish

  • Left-side driving
  • There's a black and white striped pole sign.
  • Many red and white striped bollards. They are often lined up near bridges.
  • Google Car in Bangladesh is distinctive.
  • Dhaka bank logo may be seen around town

    You may find red and white striped bollards. Often many of them line both sides of the road near bridges.

    GoogleCar in Bangladesh is distinctive and easily recognizable.

    Bengali is the main language used in Bangladesh
