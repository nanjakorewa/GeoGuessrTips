Laos

How to Distinguish

  • Cars drive on the right side.
  • License plates are often yellow.
  • The domain is .la
  • Sometimes the back of the sign is light blue.
  • Outside of Vientiane, Google Car should be visible.

Signs you can find

    License plates are yellow, and cars drive on the right side of the road . In　 Thailand, conversely, cars drive on the left side of the road.

    Lao is the official language. The stop sign is distinctive.
