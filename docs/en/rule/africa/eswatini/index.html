Eswatini

How to Distinguish

  • Domain is .sz
  • Road numbers are marked with MR.
  • Poles are often made of dark brownish thin wood.
  • The entire country is slanted with highlands (1500m elevation) in the west and lowlands (200m elevation) in the east.
  • Eswatini is divided into High Belt, Middle Belt, and Low Belt according to altitude, with different forms of agriculture.
  • Sometimes sugar cane is grown in the Lowveld.
  • In the Highveld, pine and eucalyptus forestry is practiced and plantation forests exist.
  • When you're in the Lowveld, you can often see the landscape far to the north and south, but not far enough to the east and west.

    The poles are made of dark brown thin wood, strung with one to three cords.

    It is divided into High Belt, Middle Belt, and Low Belt according to elevation, each with different forms of agriculture. Sugarcane may be grown in the Low Belt.

    Pine and eucalyptus forestry is practiced in the Highveld and plantations exist. Even if there is no forest, there may be evidence of deforestation.

    西側が高地（標高1500m）で東側が低地（標高200m）と国全体が斜めになっている。
