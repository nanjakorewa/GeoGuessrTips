Lesotho

How to Distinguish

  • The domain is .ls
  • Regarding topography, the entire country is in the Drakensberg Mountains and there are few flat areas. There are many mountains with flat tops.
  • The climate is warm and humid, with sparsely wooded grasslands.

Signs you can find

    The country exists in the Drakensberg Mountains and is generally high in elevation, with craggy ridges and flat-topped mountains . The grass is growing and sheep, cattle and other pastoralists are occasionally visible. Eswatini also has higher elevations, but in the higher elevations of Eswatini, forestry and many trees are thriving. Lesotho, on the other hand, often has no trees.

    The house has a traditional shape ( レソト - TABIPPO).
