Canary Islands

How to Distinguish

  • Spanish Outlying Islands
  • The initials of the road numbers come from the name of the island.（LP・TF・FV・GC・LZ・HI）
  • There are mountains with craggy rocky surfaces and rounded mountains.

    If you see a bollard or sign, go to the island with that initial.

    Cacti, palm trees, dragon trees ( ryuketsuju), etc. There is a mixture of various plants. The vegetation is totally different in different places Spanish mainland as well as yellow reflective bollards and guardrails.
