Hong Kong

How to Distinguish

  • Domain is .hk
  • Language is Hong Kong (dialect of Cantonese)
  • The license plate is white on the front and yellow on the back.

Signs you can find

    The license plate is white on the front and yellow on the back. If the license plate has a black background, consider the possibility of Macau.
